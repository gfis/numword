numword writes numbers (sequences of decimal digits, up to (10^6)^20) as number words in more than 30 natural languages, and converts such number words back to digit sequences. Several other entities like the names of weekdays, months and seasons are also mapped to numbers and vice versa. The word generator uses word tables and triple-digit oriented algorithms for most languages (with many exceptions, especially for far-east languages and Klingon). The number word parser uses a single general algorithm, however.
